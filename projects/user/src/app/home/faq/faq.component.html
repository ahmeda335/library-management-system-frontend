Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a book?

Simply log into your account, browse through the available books, and click the “Add to Cart” button. Once you’re ready, proceed to checkout to complete the purchase.

Do I need an account to make a purchase?

Yes, creating an account allows us to manage your orders, send receipts, and give you access to your purchase history.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order before it is shipped. Visit your “My Orders” page to make changes or contact support if needed.

Can I track my orders?

Yes! Once you place an order, you can go to the “My Orders” section in your account dashboard to view your order history, status, and details. You'll also be notified of any updates via email.

What payment methods are supported?

We currently support major payment methods including credit/debit cards and mobile wallets. More payment options will be added soon.
